SUPERFAST

Super Fast Organic Potash is a liquid organic fertilizer essential for healthy plant growth and productivity. It provides plants with the required potassium in an organic form, helping improve crop yield and overall quality. Super Fast enhances flowering, strengthens the plant structure, and boosts fruit and vegetable production. Each 1-liter bottle of Super Fast is designed to address potassium deficiencies in crops, ensuring robust growth and maximizing agricultural output across a wide range of applications.
